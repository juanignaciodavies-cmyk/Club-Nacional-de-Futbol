ATILIO GARCÍA (26/08/1914)

IMPORTANT DETAILS IN NACIONAL

He won 18 official titles, eight of them were Uruguayan championships. he is the maximium scorer in the entire history of the classics (Nacional vs Peñarol), with 35 goals. The two tournaments in which he scored the most goals were the Uruguayan championship (211 goals) and the “Campeonato de Honor” (172 goals). he played 442 matches, he won 309, he lost 70 and he tied 63 matches. He was the top scorer for eight years in the Uruguayan Championship, seven of them consecutively
